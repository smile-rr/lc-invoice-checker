  ---
  One real gap: ISBP-C10

  ISBP C10 says freight/insurance charges on a CIF invoice must not inflate the total above the LC amount AND must be consistent with the trade term. UCP-18b-amount catches the total exceeding the limit, but it
  doesn't check charge line-item consistency (e.g. a FOB invoice that also charges freight is wrong). This is an Slice 3 Type AB rule — worth adding to the catalog as a disabled placeholder now so it's visible.

  ---
  Activation trigger correctness

  Every rule's trigger is correct by design:

  - UNCONDITIONAL — UCP baseline rules that apply to every single LC regardless of wording (currency, amount, goods description, invoice date, issuer/applicant existence)
  - LC_STIPULATED — rules that only activate when the LC explicitly says so: ISBP-C1 (46A says "LC NUMBER"), ISBP-C2 (46A says "SIGNED"), ISBP-C7 (46A says "COUNTRY OF ORIGIN"), ISBP-C8 (45A has an Incoterm),
  ISBP-C4 (45A has a unit price pattern)
  - Activation never looks at the invoice — only LcDocument fields via SpEL, which is correct because activation happens before the invoice check stage

  The only thing worth adding now is ISBP-C10 as a disabled placeholder. Want me to add it?
